Core Principle
Forecasting is gated, not linear
Progress % alone does not determine readiness. A client can be 70% complete and still not be forecastable. What matters is whether the critical path is clear and the blockers are gone.
  • Progress (% complete) alone does not determine readiness
  • Critical path completion is required for forecast inclusion — data imports, product catalog
  • Unresponsive clients and incomplete data migrations are automatic disqualifiers

Continuous Improvement
💡 Key Improvements — Jan–Feb 2026
Lessons Learned
What changed and why
📐
Milestone-based inclusion replaced progress % gating. Forecast inclusion is now based on detailed milestone completion, giving more visibility into actual readiness rather than a misleading overall progress number.
🎯
Critical milestone completion is now required — not optional. Implementing a hard gate on critical path items eliminated premature forecasting and significantly improved accuracy. Data imports and product catalog must be done.
⚠️
No more relying on heroics or client responsiveness. The team needs to stop leaning on last-minute effort or optimistic client engagement assumptions. Forecast entries must be fact-based and defensible at the time of entry.
