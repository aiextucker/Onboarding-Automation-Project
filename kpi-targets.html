💡 How to Read the Baseline

The ~60-day go-live and 14–23 SA methodology scores come from the 566-call analysis (Nov 2025–Mar 2026). Several baselines are listed as "Unknown" — that's not a gap in the data, it's a gap in what we've been tracking. Setting these KPIs now means Year 1 establishes the baseline and moves the number simultaneously. The automation pipeline produces the measurement data as a byproduct of doing the work.
