PSA Graduation Criteria

Minimum requirements a client must satisfy before transitioning from Onboarding to Customer Success.

Version v1.2 — Draft
Created May 7, 2026
Status Pending review with Will

A client graduates when they can independently work tickets and bill customers accurately within Rev.io PSA. All items below must be checked before a CS handoff is initiated. Graduation should not be granted under schedule pressure if requirements are not met.

Completion 0 of 16 criteria met
👤
1. User Management & Permissions Required
At least one non-admin user created
Client has added a technician or billing user beyond the default admin account.
User Mgmt
At least one permission set configured and assigned
Role-based access confirmed — users have appropriate permissions for their function.
User Mgmt
📦
2. Product Catalog Required
Products and services loaded into the catalog
Active services the client sells are present in PSA with correct names and pricing.
Config
🏢
3. Customer Import & Setup Required
Customer data imported or manually entered
Client's customer base is live in PSA — either via data import or manual entry.
Config
Created at least one customer record from start to finish
Client (or SA with client present) completed the full customer creation workflow including contacts and billing settings.
Training
⚙️
4. System Settings Required
Bill Profile(s) configured and activated
Billing cycle, cycle dates, arrears settings, and grace period are set and confirmed. Build profile is active.
ConfigBilling
Email configuration completed
Outbound email is configured and tested — invoices, notifications, and ticket emails route correctly.
Config
Standard workflow configured ⚠ TBD — to be defined with team
The agreed-upon standard workflow every PSA client needs is in place and confirmed working.
Config
🧾
5. Tax Configuration Required
Tax mappings loaded
Tax jurisdictions are configured in PSA. SureTax activated and credentials entered (if applicable).
Billing
Taxes appear on a generated invoice
A test or draft invoice shows tax line items populated. Accuracy of rates is outside onboarding scope.
Billing
✅
6. Ticketing & Billing Validation Required
Client has created and worked at least one ticket end-to-end
Ticket opened, assigned, updated, and closed. Client understands the ticket lifecycle in PSA.
Training
At least one invoice created
Client has generated a complete invoice. Delivery to the end customer is not required at this stage.
Billing
Client can independently navigate ticketing and billing workflows
SA has assessed that the client's team can operate PSA without hand-holding for their core daily tasks.
Training
🔗
7. Integrations If Applicable
QuickBooks Online (QBO) integration connected and error-free
QBO sync is enabled and the error log is clean — no failed sync events. Activation alone is not sufficient; confirm the integration is actively passing data without errors.
ConfigVerify Logs
Calendar sync connected and confirmed
Calendar integration (e.g. Google Calendar, Microsoft 365) is active and syncing correctly. Required for any client using dispatch. Verify at least one technician's calendar is linked and reflecting PSA schedule.
ConfigDispatch
SA Sign-Off
