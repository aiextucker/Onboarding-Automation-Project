Key Observations

🔴 Functionality is the #1 churn driver

25 of 38 cancels (66%) are "Canceled – Functionality." Add 7 RTS-Functionality and 7 On Hold-Functionality and you have 39 clients impacted by product gap issues. This is a Sales expectation problem, not an onboarding problem.

🔴 November 2025 — worst cohort

5 of 9 clients sold in November canceled (56%). Worth reviewing who the Sales reps were that month and whether deal quality or client fit was an issue. Small sample but the worst rate in the dataset.

🟡 RTS clients are not counted as canceled — yet

17 clients in RTS status are technically "stuck." If even half eventually cancel, the real stick rate for mature cohorts drops another 5–8 points. RTS-Ghosting (9 clients) is the highest-risk sub-bucket.

🟡 Mature cohort stick rate: ~62%

Ignoring the immature 2026 months, Jul–Nov 2025 (the cohorts with real data) average a 58% stick rate. The overall 72% figure is inflated by early-stage clients who haven't had time to cancel yet.

🟢 Oct 2025 is the standout

18 sold, only 4 canceled, 78% stick rate — best among mature cohorts. Worth examining what was different about deal quality or SA execution that month.

🟢 NKO is scheduling lag, not churn risk

5 NKO clients are awaiting kickoff scheduling — not stalled or at-risk. Once scheduled they move to active pipeline. Track them separately from RTS/On Hold to avoid overstating the stall rate.
